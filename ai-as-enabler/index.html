AI as an Enabler

Use AI as part of the bigger event picture: start from the goal, then turn interaction, knowledge and content into measurable audience value.

1

Goal first

Define what the event must achieve: leads, ROI, engagement, brand impact or deeper audience insight.

ActionWrite one success sentence + 2–3 metrics before choosing any AI tool.
2

Interactive AI host

Use avatars or assistants to make content interactive before, during and after the event.

ActionPick the moments where attendees need guidance, Q&A, onboarding or follow-up.
3

Distil & visualize

Transform talks, responses and takeaways into summaries, infographics, videos and visual assets.

ActionPlan capture points: transcripts, polls, interviews, feedback and content outputs.
4

Capture tacit knowledge

Surface themes, needs and insights from conversations, workshops and audience behavior.

ActionAssign who collects insights, where they land, and how they are reviewed.
5

Build custom tools

Create lightweight apps and automations for attendees, organizers or behind-the-scenes teams.

ActionPrototype only tools that remove friction or make the defined goal easier to reach.
6

Personalize value

Give each attendee relevant recaps, highlights, matches, content and post-event next steps.

ActionSegment audiences and define what each group should receive after the event.
7

Create memorable moments

Use AI to make experiences participatory, surprising and immersive — without losing the human idea.

ActionDesign one “talked-about” AI moment that strengthens the brand and event story.
