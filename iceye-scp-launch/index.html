Recommended strategic angle

SCP should be framed as the missing command layer of sovereign ISR.

This should not feel like a product demo or a generic webinar. It should feel like a focused decision environment for each buyer layer.

“SCP turns distributed satellite capacity into coordinated operational capability — with clear tasking, prioritisation and authority preserved.”

A strong category claim, without implying that sovereign authority moves away from national actors.

Satellite capacity
ICEYE’s full-stack constellation and tactical ISR capability, available now.
Mission software
The operational layer that connects tasking, data flows and mission execution.
SCP allocation layer
How ISR capacity is prioritised and allocated across domains, users and allied needs.
National authority
How control, permissions and accountability stay clear in federated use.
Why now

The market is moving from satellite access to operational control.

More sensors do not automatically create better decisions. SCP gives ICEYE a way to define the shift from access to satellites toward operational control.

Recommended platform logic

One shared signal. Three tailored virtual rooms.

Use an event platform that can support role-specific virtual rooms. The shared programme can appear inside each room, so audiences do not need to experience a visible switch between groups.
