Recommended strategic angle

SCP should be framed as the allocation layer of sovereign ISR.

The strongest launch is not a product demonstration first. It is a decision-making format that helps Europe understand why federated satellite capacity needs a single operating layer — without diluting national authority.

“SCP turns distributed satellite capacity into a single operational asset under one chain of command.”

This is the category claim. Everything in the launch should make it visible, credible and usable for different buyer layers.

Satellite capacity
ICEYE’s full-stack constellation and tactical ISR capability: available now, not years from delivery.
Mission software
The operational foundation that connects tasking, data flows and mission execution.
SCP allocation layer
The missing command layer: allocate distributed sovereign capacity across domains, users and allied needs.
National authority
Federation without merger: allied capacity can be shared while sovereign control remains intact.
Three buyer layers

One category. Three different reasons to believe.

The launch should use one shared message but not one generic script. Each buyer layer needs to hear the SCP argument through its own decision responsibility.

01 / Economic buyers

Political leadership & EU institutions

  • Why federated sovereign capacity matters now
  • Why federation protects rather than dilutes authority
  • Why ICEYE is available now, not in a future programme
02 / Technical buyers

Military decision-makers & space command

  • How allocation across domains actually works
  • How SCP sits above mission software
  • How command chain and operational control stay clear
03 / User buyers

SOC staff & ISR officers

  • What changes in tasking, priority and decision flow
  • How the interface supports operational judgement
  • Why users can advocate internally after seeing it

Risk / caveat

The biggest risk is a generic webinar.

If the same script tries to serve all three audiences, the message becomes too abstract for operators, too shallow for technical buyers and too technical for political buyers. The launch needs one shared category claim — but three distinct paths to belief.
