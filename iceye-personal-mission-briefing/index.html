What SCP means for you

SCP helps turn distributed satellite capacity into one operational allocation layer. For a technical buyer, the central question is not only what ICEYE can see, but how capacity is requested, prioritised and controlled across users.

Federation needs rulesShared capacity only works if access, permissions and national authority are designed into the operating model.

The interface is strategicThe daily UI is where policy, mission urgency and satellite availability become real decisions.
